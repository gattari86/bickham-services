Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Why Wix?

Enterprise-grade security, easy for your team to manage post-launch, and fully scalable as the business grows.

Will my current site go down during development?

No. Everything is built in a private test environment. Your current site stays live with zero downtime until the new one is approved.

What if I need changes after launch?

Options 2 and 3 include post-launch optimization. Option 1 clients can add support later at any time.

Do I need to provide content?

We handle the copy framework. You provide business details, testimonials, and brand inputs during kickoff.

What’s the difference between Option 2 and 3?

Option 2 covers the first 3 months post-launch—the most hands-on phase, when the site needs the most active refinement. That intensity is reflected in the monthly rate. Option 3 extends support to 6 months at a lower monthly rate, allowing improvements to compound as the site stabilizes.

What does “AI (LLM/Agent)-Ready” mean?

We structure your content so AI tools like ChatGPT, Google AI, and business search agents can accurately understand and surface your services. This is how people are increasingly finding businesses.
